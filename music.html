Random Practice

Percussion Ensemble

As the title might suggest, this is my first serious exploration with musical composition. After beginning with light, common duple rhythms, I took my first dive into odd-meters, pusing the boundaries of my knowledge, groove, and roles of percussion instrumentation.

Marching Band

These drumline parts were composed for a marching band arrangment of "Thriller" for the BEA Marching Band in 2014. A fusion of dubstep and funk grooves are used throughout to put some salt on Michael Jackson's sweet and well-known melody.
